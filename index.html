Welcome to RPS international school

At RPS, education is a journey that ignites imagination through enriching learning experiences in a child-friendly, safe, and nurturing environment. We believe that learning becomes enjoyable when its rooted in sensory experiences that inspire exploration, innovation, and creativity. With our "play, learn, and discover" approach, we cultivate a stimulating atmosphere that offers countless opportunities to develop innate talents while fostering social, emotional, psychomotor, and ethical skills.
